Check if you’re self-employed

Contractors need to check their employment status as it will affect the way they pay tax.

Contactors may have to change the tax they pay if they’re classed as an employee by HM Revenue and Customs. They may be taxed as an employee even if they work through an intermediary or as a sole trader.

This tool will ask you questions to help you work out if:

  • a contractor is employed or self-employed
  • intermediaries legislation (IR35) applies to the contractor’s limited company

You can check someone’s employment status if you are the:

  • contractor providing a service
  • person or company hiring a contractor
  • agency that are placing a contractor

To check someone’s employment status, you will need to know the:

  • type of company they work for
  • terms of the contract
