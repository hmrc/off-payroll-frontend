The criteria would include:

  • being equally skilled, qualified, security cleared and able to perform the worker’s duties
  • not being interviewed by the end client before they start (except for verification checks)
  • not being from a pool or bank of workers regularly engaged by the end client
  • doing all of the worker’s tasks for that period of time
  • being substituted because the worker is unwilling or unable to do the work

We need to know what would happen in practice, not just what it says in the worker’s contract.
