Associates include: spouse or civil partner, linear descendents or antecedents, business partner, trustees of a trust in which you are the settlor, trustees of a trust in which you are the beneficiary, settlor of a trust in which you are a beneficiary. The more contracts you've had for other clients in the past 12 months, the more likely you are to be working like a business. This means it's less likely that, in HMRC's view, you should pay tax on payroll.
