Public sector

If you’re the worker you should tell the organisation that pays your fees (the fee payer) to deduct tax and National Insurance from your payment.

If you’re the fee payer you need to deduct tax and National Insurance from the worker’s deemed employment payment(s) during this engagement.

If you’re the end client, and not the organisation paying the worker (fee payer). You should tell the fee paying agency to deduct tax and National Insurance from the worker’s deemed employment payment(s) during this engagement.
